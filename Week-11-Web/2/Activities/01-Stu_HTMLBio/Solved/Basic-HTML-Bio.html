Leia Organa

Leia Skywalker Organa Solo is a Force-sensitive human female political and military leader who served in the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Imperial Era and the New Republic and Resistance in the subsequent New Republic Era.

Adopted into the House of Organa, the Alderaanian royal family, she is Princess Leia Organa of Alderaan, a planet in the Core Worlds known for its dedication to pacifism.
